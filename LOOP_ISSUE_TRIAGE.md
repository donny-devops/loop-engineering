# Issue Triage Loop

## Schedule
- Every 2 hours during active sprints; 4h otherwise

## Scope
- Open issues in tracker
- Rolling backlog health

## Safety Gates
- No auto-label or close in L1.
- Escalate security and ambiguous items.

## Skills
- `issue-triage` — scans and prioritizes issues

## State
- `issue-triage-state.md` — rolling backlog health

## Human Handoff
- Security/auth items immediately.
- Stale items open >14 days.

## Cost & Limits
- 2h cadence cap: 25k tokens per run
- No auto-apply in L1

## Observability
- Log runs to `loop-run-log.md`.
